James Morgan "Jimmy" McGill, also known by his business name Saul Goodman and later by the alias Gene Takavic,[2] is a fictional character who appears in the television series Breaking Bad and serves as the titular protagonist of its spin-off prequel/sequel series Better Call Saul. He is primarily portrayed by Bob Odenkirk, and was created by Vince Gilligan and Peter Gould.
